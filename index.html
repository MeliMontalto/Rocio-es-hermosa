    Past Simple Past Simple
    Used to express a complete action/habit in the past.


    Affirmative form:
    I watched tv.
    He/She/It watched tv.
    We/They watched tv.

    Negative form:
    I didn't watch tv.
    He/She/It didn't watch tv.
    We/They didn't watch tv.

    Question form:
    Did I/He/She/It/We/They watch tv?
    Yes, I/He/She/It/We/They did.
    No, I/He/She/It/We/They didn't.
